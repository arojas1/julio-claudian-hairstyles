## Imperator Caesar Divi Filius Augustus (a.k.a Augustus)
<br>

### Background
Augustus was the first Roman emperor, taking over after his great-uncle and adoptive father, Julius Ceaser.[1] He ruled from 27 B.C until his death in A.D 14.

Augustus, being the first Roman emperor, was the one to set the precedent for the Julio-Claudian portraitures and hairstyles. This is evident in the fact that none of the portraits throughout Augustus' reign portray him in old age (that have been found), despite him living until he was 77 years old.[2]
